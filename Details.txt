The Convocation Day:
Description:
How surprising & Full of pleasure is the day when you get graduate and get your degree. You wish every your loved one should be there. But you miss lot of them. Now with the help of this application, you can invite your loved one at your convocation day with just a single click and by sending the nice convocation invitation card. 
	
Features:
•	The series of high quality Convocation invitation cards.
•	Categorized cards to easily classify your party invitation.
•	A User Form to fill out the information about party event.
•	One-Click sending and sharing functionality.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing)
